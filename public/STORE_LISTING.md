## Full Description

Expense Manager is a clean, fast, and private personal finance app designed to
help you stay on top of your daily spending — all stored locally on your device,
with zero cloud dependency.

**KEY FEATURES**

- Track credits and debits with mandatory remarks for full context
- View your current balance at a glance on the beautiful home dashboard
- Daily reports: see where your money went, day by day
- Built-in currency converter supporting 50+ world currencies
  (USD, INR, IDR, PHP, JPY, EUR, GBP and 43 more)
- Export all your data as a versioned JSON backup and re-import anytime
- Two stunning themes: Nature (warm beige + green) and Dark Mode
- Full multi-language support: English, Hindi, Japanese, Indonesian, German
- 100% offline — your data never leaves your device

**PRIVACY FIRST**
No accounts, no servers, no ads. Everything is stored locally using AsyncStorage.

**DESIGNED FOR EVERYONE**
Whether you are tracking grocery spending, managing household budgets, or simply
keeping a record of daily expenses, Expense Manager is built to be intuitive and
fast.
